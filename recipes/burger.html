Burger

Description

Today we're gonna make the best and most delicious Burger ever with my extra special recipe for a simple hamburger.

Nothing beats a good hamburger.

Ingrededients

  • 500g of ground beef
  • 2 whole Onions
  • burger buns
  • meltable cheese slices
  • jalapenos
  • tomatoes
  • salad
  • hot bbq sauce
  1. First cut one of your onions into cubes and mix them well with your grounded beef
  2. Prepare your tomatoes , jalapenos, onion and salad so they are ready for your burger
  3. Cut the tomatoes, jalapenos, onion into small slices and rip some salad off
  4. Next heat your grill and put some butter on it.
  5. Then form your ground beef into a burger patty and put it onto the grill
  6. Put the cutted jalapenos and onion on the grill and let it sit there for some time
  7. Take the jalapenos out pretty fast but let the onions stay longer until they're brown
  8. When your burger patty is about done put some cheese slices on it and let it melt.
  9. By the time its melted the onions should be done. Now put everything aside and prepare for making your burger
  10. First sauce, then the salad, then the burger patty, then the jalapenos and tomatoes and salad again.
  11. Now put some sauce on the top bun and the onions on top of the sauce so they stick to it
  12. Congratulations you made your burger
