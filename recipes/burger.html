Burger

Description

Today we're gonna make the best and most delicious Burger ever with my extra special recipe for a simple hamburger.

Nothing beats a good hamburger.

Ingredients

  • 500g of ground beef
  • 2 whole Onions
  • burger buns
  • meltable cheese slices
  • jalapenos
  • tomatoes
  • salad
  • hot bbq sauce
  1. First cut one of your onions into cubes and mix them well with your ground beef.
  2. Prepare your tomatoes , jalapenos, your second onion and salad so they are ready for your burger.
  3. Cut the tomatoes, jalapenos, onion into small slices and rip some salad off.
  4. Next heat your grill and put some butter on it.
  5. Then form your ground beef into a burger patty and put it onto the grill.
  6. Put the cut jalapenos and onion on the grill and let it sit there for some time.
  7. First take the jalapenos off the grill and then the onions as well once they're caramelized.
  8. When your burger patty is about as done as you'd like it, put some cheese slices on it and let them melt.
  9. Put everything aside and prepare to make your burger.
  10. First sauce, then the salad, then the burger patty, then the jalapenos and tomatoes and salad again.
  11. Now put some sauce on the top bun and the onions on top of the sauce so they stick to it.
  12. Congratulations you made your burger.
